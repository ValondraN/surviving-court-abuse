About

A trauma-informed educational project for people navigating high-conflict family court dynamics.

Purpose

This site exists to support healing and clarity for people who feel harmed by court conflict. It focuses on education, emotional resilience, and practical tools that reduce overwhelm.

Content is intentionally general and privacy-first. No names. No case details. No real-time reactions.

Disclaimer: This site provides general educational information and is not legal advice.

What this site is not

It is not a place to post accusations, documents, or timelines. It is not a substitute for legal counsel.

What you can request

Templates and scripts, suggested topics, and practical guides. Use the Contact page to request resources.
